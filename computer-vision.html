Vision AI / Inspection

Computer Vision System

Camera-based intelligence for detection, counting, quality checks, safety monitoring, site inspection, and visual automation.

Computer vision becomes valuable when it is connected to a workflow. AXIS does not stop at a model demo. We build capture, labeling, detection, review, alerts, dashboards, and improvement loops.

Inputs
Cameras, video files, drones, CCTV, mobile uploads
Tasks
Detect, count, classify, measure, inspect
Review
Human approval and false-positive correction
Output
Alerts, reports, dashboards, APIs
01

Data capture

Collect images and video from the right angles, lighting, distance, and resolution for reliable detection.

02

Labeling

Create a clean training dataset with object labels, edge cases, and examples of what should be ignored.

03

Model pipeline

Train, test, or integrate models for the specific inspection, counting, or detection task.

04

Review tools

Let people verify detections, correct mistakes, and improve the dataset over time.

05

Alerts

Trigger notifications when a threshold, hazard, missing item, or quality issue appears.

06

Dashboards

Show trends, detection history, site comparisons, and operational reports.
