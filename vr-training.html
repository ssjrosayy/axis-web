VR Training System

Interactive simulations for safety training, equipment walkthroughs, site orientation, product demos, and high-risk procedures.

VR is useful when training is expensive, dangerous, repetitive, or hard to explain with slides. AXIS builds the scene, interaction logic, scoring, admin dashboard, and deployment path.

Users
Trainees, instructors, sales teams
Modules
3D scene, interactions, scoring, analytics
Devices
Meta Quest, desktop, web preview
Output
Training results and performance reports
01

Scenario design

Turn the real training process into clear scenes, decisions, tasks, hazards, and success criteria.

02

3D environment

Build or optimize equipment, rooms, vehicles, product models, and guided interaction zones.

03

Interaction logic

Let users pick up objects, follow steps, trigger errors, repeat tasks, and receive feedback.

04

Assessment

Track completion time, mistakes, missed steps, safety issues, and instructor notes.

05

Admin dashboard

Give managers results, progress, pass rates, and trainee history.

06

Deployment

Package the experience for headsets, internal demos, onboarding rooms, or web-based previews.
